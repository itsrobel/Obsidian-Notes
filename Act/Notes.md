# Math
## Solving Equations

___Isolate Variables___
use inverse operations:
$mult <-> div$
$add <-> sub$


___solving "in terms of"___
A _Variable_ will be a part of the final expression
_e.g._ x in terms of y:

$$x = 2y-3$$


___Word Problems___
1) Label _Variables_
2) ID what to solve
3) Translate words into algebra, one step at a time
